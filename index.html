If you’re anything like me, your virtual universe is inundated with Clarendon-filtered selfies, boomeranged birthday cakes being blown and unblown, or the latest from a certain orange “politician”.

This is not that.

12x is an experimental attempt to capture the discussions we have in our private lives and... internet them. One-to-many (well, like three of you) communication with the flavour of one-to-one.

What's this about?

Mainly, the stuff we already talk about. Big, small and mid-size abstract ideas that have been on my mind, what I'm working on at that moment in time, cool design stuff I think you might like, good tunes to listen to, and a few (I promise, just a few) links to interesting articles or videos online.

I won't spam you.

Honestly, one email a month is going to be enough of a challenge. I have nothing to sell you (yet), I just wanna talk.

It takes two to tango

The whole point in me doing this is to spark conversation. So I need you. If you want to talk about something in an email, just hit reply. If you know someone who might like any of the topics, forward it. If I'm doing a horrible job, or there's something you'd like to see in a future email, let me know. I might even run a little survey eventually.

P.S.

If the monospace font is annoying, I'm sorry. It's an experiment. Trying to get away from my usual style. It might change.

who dis?

I’m Nuff, a designer type. I've spent most of the past decade drawing faceless portraits, designing minimalist logos, and building UI.

In 2016, I suffered burnout and have been struggling to get creative projects off the ground ever since. 12x is a super-personal therapy project aimed at lowerfing the stakes and restoring creative confidence.
